My favorite city

Besides the city where I was born, my favorite city in this world, in my opinion, is Riga.

Riga - as my second hometown

I have learned Latvian as my major for 4 years. During my studies, I learned about the history, the culture and other aspects of Latvia and its capital city - Riga. Besides, to imrove my language skills, I have benn in Latvia 2 times - first time for attending the summer school organized by the University of Latvia, and the second time as an exchange student in RTU for one year.

Although, compared to many cities in my motherland, the history of Riga is not as long as theirs, Riga attracted me by its mysterious stories of its establishment, the unique landscape of the OLD Riga, as well as the river "Daugava", which clears my heart whenever I walk by it.
